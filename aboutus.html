About Us

Who We Are

Singha Security Solutions (Pvt) Ltd (SSS) provides top-notch security services to a diverse range of clients, including private households, multinational companies, and government organizations. We prioritize listening to our customers to meet their needs professionally and promptly. Our team of well-trained, highly professional, and experienced employees, many of whom are ex-service personnel, ensures absolute professionalism and efficiency in protecting property, assets, and people.

Vision

"To be the most professional and reliable security solutions partner in the industry by exceeding our customer's expectations while valuing our employees."

Mission

"To provide innovative security solutions with high quality standards in order to help our clients in preventing losses of life, property, assets and the environment."

Our Leadership

Mr. Eranda Singhabahu

Managing Director

A decorated Army veteran with expertise in defense operations, security management, and human resources. He served courageously during Sri Lanka's critical conflict period, leading offensive operations against the LTTE and earning multiple commendations.

A retired officer of the Sri Lanka Singha Regiment, he holds an MBA (Human Resources), BA in Defense Management, and a Diploma in HRM. Post-retirement, he transitioned to senior managerial and consultancy roles, leveraging his military leadership and HR expertise to support organizations in security and workforce management. His career includes participation in high-stakes military campaigns and specialized training programs both locally and internationally.
