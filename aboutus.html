About Us

Your trusted partner in safety and security.

Who We Are

Singha Security Solutions (Pvt) Ltd (SSS) provides top-notch security services to a diverse range of clients, including private households, multinational companies, and government organizations. We prioritize listening to our customers to meet their needs professionally and promptly. Our team of well-trained, highly professional, and experienced employees, many of whom are ex-service personnel, ensures absolute professionalism and efficiency in protecting property, assets, and people.

Our Values

Customer Focus

We have loyal and open relationships with our customers that generate trust to ensure the mutual benefits of our organizations as well as for customers.

Expertise

We develop and demonstrate our expertise through our innovative, leading cutting edge approach to creating and delivering the right security solution.

Performance

We challenge ourselves to improve performance on a day to day basis in order to create long-term sustainability.

Best People

We take care to employ the best quality people, develop their competencies through skills transfer programs, provide further opportunities within the organization and inspire them to perform in accordance with our company values.

Teamwork

We collaborate to ensure the success of ourselves, our staff and our customers.

Integrity

We can be consistently relied upon to practice high ethical and professional standards in our service delivery.

Our Leadership

Maj (Rtd) Eranda Singhabahu

Managing Director

A decorated Army veteran with expertise in defense operations, security management, and human resources. He served courageously during Sri Lanka’s critical conflict period, leading offensive operations against the LTTE and earning multiple commendations.

A retired Major of the Sri Lanka Singha Regiment, he holds an MBA (Human Resources), BA in Defense Management, and a Diploma in HRM. Post-retirement, he transitioned to senior managerial and consultancy roles, leveraging his military leadership and HR expertise to support organizations in security and workforce management. His career includes participation in high-stakes military campaigns and specialized training programs both locally and internationally.
