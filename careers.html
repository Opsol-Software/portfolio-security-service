Officer In Charge (OIC)

General Responsibilities:

  • Monitoring surveillance equipment
  • Maintaining an activity log and preparing surveillance reports
  • Inspecting buildings, equipment, and access points, permitting entry and exit
  • Monitoring and authorizing entrance and departure of visitors
  • Responding to alarms and analyzing security footage
  • Check surveillance conforming periodically to identify disturbances or unlawful acts
  • Awareness on Quality Policy and objectives, Contribution to the effectiveness of QMS
  • Application of not conforming to the QMS requirements/ other compliances

Junior Security Officer (JSO)

General Responsibilities:

  • Ensure all employees and visitors have proper credentials for accessing buildings (ID badges and passes)
  • Assist employees and visitors with any concerns they might have
  • Inspecting buildings, equipment, accessing and existing points
  • Complete reports by recording observations, information, occurrences, and surveillance activities
  • Ensure the security, safety and well-being of all personnel, visitors and the premises
  • Controls traffic by directing drivers

Lady Security Officer (LSO)

General Responsibilities:

  • Ensure all employees and visitors have proper credentials for accessing buildings (ID badges and passes)
  • Assist employees and visitors with any concerns they might have
  • Complete reports by recording observations, information, occurrences, and surveillance activities
  • Ensure the security, safety and well-being of all personnel, visitors and the premises
